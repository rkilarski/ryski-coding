About the New England Valkyries RFC

Our Mission

The New England Valkyries Rugby Football Club is a gay rugby club dedicated to creating an open and inclusive environment, based on mutual respect, designed to provide intensive basic training for adult beginners as well as challenging training for experienced ruggers, with the goal of participating in competitive rugby and challenging homophobia in sport.

Who we are

The Valkyries are, first and foremost, a band of rugby brothers. Every member of our club is focused on competitive rugby before everything else - players, coach and leaders. We work hard in training, we leave everything on the pitch and then we play hard. Our debut this year at the Combustion Tournament in Chicago put this philosophy to the test and we were rewarded with two days of amazing rugby and a second place trophy.

What we do

The Valkyries rugby program is designed to instill a basic core of rugby skills and fitness for those who have never played rugby, while at the same time maintaining a challenging training program for those more experienced with the sport. If you're new to the sport we know starting out in rugby can be overwhelming. Our head coach, with almost 20 years of rugby playing and coaching experience has designed a Rugby 101 program to get you up to speed on all the basics - skills, terminology and basic conditioning, before you're integrated into regular practice sessions. After graduation from the Rugby 101 program, new players will be paired with an experienced player who will help them get up to speed on regular practice sessions. Our goal is to give every player, new and experienced, every tool and resource they need to improve their level of rugby with every training session.

Who can join?

The Valkyries are a gay rugby club, but you don't have to be gay to be a member. We believe that rugby should be for anyone with the passion to embrace the game. Our club welcomes players from all races, sexual orientations and skill levels.

If you're looking for a physically and mentally challenging sport, in an environment of hard work and mutual respect than the Valkyries is the team for you. Join Us!
