About the New England Valkyries Rugby Club

Our Mission

The New England Valkyries Rugby Football Club is a gay rugby club (mainly based in Boston, but with presence throughout New England) dedicated to creating an open and inclusive environment, based on mutual respect, designed to provide intensive basic training for adult beginners as well as challenging training for experienced ruggers, with the goal of participating in competitive rugby and challenging homophobia in sport.
