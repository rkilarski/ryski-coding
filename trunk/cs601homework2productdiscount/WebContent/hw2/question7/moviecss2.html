The Princess Bride

The Princess Bride is a story of true love, adventure, excitement, danger, chases, escapes. Miracles. It's a story of Buttercup, the most beautiful woman in the world, who loses her true love, Westley, at sea. So she agrees to marry evil Prince Humperdink, until she is kidnapped by three very strange people...

Starring:
  • Cary Elwes as Westley
  • Robin Wright as Buttercup, The Princess Bride
  • Mandy Patinkin as Inigo Montoya
  • Andre the Giant as Fezzik
  • Wallace Shawn as Vizzini
