Guitariffic allows you to create guitar fake sheets. Just drag and drop chords from the chord tray at the right into the chord area, add some lyrics and a song name, and you're good to go!


Make sure you check out these cool features:


  • Edit any chord by clicking on it!
  • Add a new chord to the database by clicking on the '+' button next to the search bar!
  • Add your favorite artist in the Artist field and watch the background!
